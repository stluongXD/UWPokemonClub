Who We Are

The Pokemon Club at UW is a club created by Pokemon fans at the University of Washington. Our goal as a club is to create a welcoming space for all Pokemon fans to enjoy the various mediums of Pokemon. We welcome all students of various familiarity of Pokemon such as Pokemon Go players, Trading Card Game Players, anime only watchers, or even students who just want to learn more about Pokemon. Our events include, Pokemon battle tournaments, trivia games, watch parties and more!
